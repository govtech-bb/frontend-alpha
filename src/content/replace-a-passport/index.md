# Replace a Passport

Use this service to apply for a replacement Barbados passport if your current passport is:

- Lost
- Stolen
- Damaged
- Expired

## Before you start

You will need:

- Your current (or most recent) passport number
- Your contact information (email and phone number)
- Your delivery address in Barbados
- A payment method for the replacement fee ($150 BBD)

## What happens next

After you submit your application and payment:

1. The Immigration Department will receive your application immediately
2. You will receive an email confirmation
3. The department will contact you within 2-3 business days with next steps
4. You may need to visit the Immigration Department in person with supporting documents

## Supporting Documents

Depending on your reason for replacement, you may need to bring:

**For lost or stolen passports:**
- Police report
- Proof of citizenship (birth certificate or certificate of naturalization)
- Two recent passport-sized photos

**For damaged passports:**
- Your damaged passport
- Two recent passport-sized photos

**For expired passports:**
- Your expired passport
- Two recent passport-sized photos

## Fees

The replacement fee is **$150.00 BBD** (non-refundable).

Payment is processed securely through the Government of Barbados EZPay+ payment gateway. You can pay using:

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard)
- Visa or Mastercard debit card
- Direct debit from your bank account (takes 5 business days to process)
- Payce Digital mobile payment

## Processing Time

Standard processing time is **6-8 weeks** from the date we receive your supporting documents.

Expedited service may be available for an additional fee - contact the Immigration Department for details.
