# Apply for a Passport Replacement

Before you start your application, make sure you have:

- Your current (or most recent) passport number
- Your email address and phone number
- Your delivery address in Barbados  
- A payment method for the $150 BBD fee

## Payment Information

The application fee is **$150.00 BBD** (non-refundable).

You can pay securely using:
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard)
- Visa or Mastercard debit card  
- Direct debit from your bank account (processing takes 5 business days)
- Payce Digital mobile payment

Payment is processed through the Government of Barbados EZPay+ secure payment gateway.

## What You'll Need to Do

1. Complete the application form with your details
2. Review your information
3. Pay the application fee securely
4. Receive email confirmation

After we receive your application, the Immigration Department will contact you within 2-3 business days with instructions on submitting your supporting documents.

## Supporting Documents

Depending on your reason for replacement, you'll need to bring documents to the Immigration Department:

**For lost or stolen passports:**
- Police report
- Proof of citizenship (birth certificate or certificate of naturalization)
- Two recent passport-sized photos

**For damaged passports:**
- Your damaged passport
- Two recent passport-sized photos

**For expired passports:**
- Your expired passport
- Two recent passport-sized photos

## Privacy Notice

Your personal information will be used only to process your passport replacement application and will be handled in accordance with Barbados data protection laws.

Form data is temporarily stored in your browser and is not saved on our servers.
