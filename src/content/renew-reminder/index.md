Use this service to set a free reminder before your driver's licence, vehicle registration, passport, or other government permit runs out.

It takes less than a minute.

<a data-start-link href="/travel-id-citizenship/renew-reminder/form">Start now</a>

## Before you start

You will need:

- the type of document (for example, driver's licence or passport)
- the expiry date shown on your document

## What we ask for

To set up a reminder we only ask for the type of document and its expiry date.

We do not send you any messages and we do not keep a copy of your reminder. You save it to your own calendar.

We do not ask for your name, address, date of birth, or any document numbers. Read our [privacy notice](https://alpha.gov.bb/terms-conditions).
