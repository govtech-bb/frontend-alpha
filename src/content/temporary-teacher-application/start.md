Use this form to apply to be a temporary or acting teacher in Barbados.
 
## What you will need
• Your personal details
• Your Barbados Identification (ID) number or passport number
• Your National Insurance Number (NIS)
• Your permanent address
• Your education history
• Your qualifications
• Your work experience
• Contact details for two references
• A zipped folder containing your certificates, diplomas, and transcripts
• Two testimonials

## Documents you must upload:
• Certificates, diplomas, and transcripts (zipped folder)
• Two testimonials
 
Make sure your files are clear and easy to read.
