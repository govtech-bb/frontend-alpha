The Directorate, Learning and Development have responsibility for:

  * Learning and Development 
  * Performance Management 
  * Competency Management and Assessment 
  * Career Planning 
  * Talent Management; and 
  * Administration of: 

\- Training leave 

\- Study leave 

\- Applications for training loans.
