You can register for the Youth Development Programme (YDP) Community Sports Training programme online. The application should take around 10 to 15 minutes to complete.

Once you submit your application, it will be reviewed and you will be contacted about next steps.

## Information you will need

To complete the registration, you will need:

- Your personal details (name, date of birth, gender)

- Your sport discipline of interest

- Information about your sports experience (if any)

- Your employment status and institution details (if applicable)

- Organization membership information (if applicable)

- Your contact information (address, parish, phone number)

- Emergency contact details (name, address, parish, phone number)
