[Job Start Plus](https://labour.gov.bb/jobstartplus/) is a training programme for young people aimed at preparing them for the workplace. It offers career guidance and tailored training to develop relevant skills and build confidence. There’s also the opportunity to be placed in a place of work.

## Eligibility

You are eligible to sign up if you meet both of the 2 criteria:

1. You are 16 to 24 years old (or between 16 and 34 if you are a person with a disability).
2. You are not currently employed or enrolled in a training institution.

## About the training

‘World of Work’ training workshops are held in person over 3 weeks at a central location. Over those 3 weeks, job starters should be available between 8.45am and 4.30pm for 5 days a week.

The workshops focus on workforce preparation and core development skills such as:

- resume writing
- job search strategies
- interview techniques
- effective communication
- teamwork
- conflict resolution
- anger management
- time management
- work ethics

Job starters are given a stipend to cover lunch and travel expenses.

## When to register

Registration is open between March and April each year. The training begins in May.

## After you have registered

Once your registration has been processed, you will be sent the schedule and what you will need to bring. A session may be scheduled with a psychologist so they can assess your cognitive abilities.
