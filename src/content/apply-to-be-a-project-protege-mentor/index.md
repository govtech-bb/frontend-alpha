The Project Protégé mentor programme provides experienced professionals the opportunity to guide and support individuals in their professional development journey.

As a mentor, you will share your knowledge, experience, and insights to help mentees develop their skills, navigate career challenges, and achieve their professional goals.

---

## About the programme

Project Protégé connects mentees with experienced professionals who can provide guidance, advice, and support. Mentors play a crucial role in:

- Sharing professional knowledge and expertise
- Providing career guidance and advice
- Supporting skill development
- Offering networking opportunities
- Building confidence and professional capabilities

---

## Mentor commitment

As a mentor, you will be expected to:

- Meet with your mentee regularly (frequency to be agreed)
- Provide constructive feedback and guidance
- Share relevant professional experiences
- Support your mentee's development goals
- Maintain confidentiality and professional boundaries

---

## Who can apply

We welcome applications from professionals who:

- Have relevant professional experience to share
- Are committed to supporting others' development
- Can commit time to regular mentoring sessions
- Have strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Are passionate about developing talent

---

## How to apply

Complete the online application form. You will need to provide:

- Your personal and contact details
- Information about your professional experience
- Your mentoring preferences
- Professional and personal references

Applications are reviewed regularly, and successful applicants will be contacted to discuss next steps.
