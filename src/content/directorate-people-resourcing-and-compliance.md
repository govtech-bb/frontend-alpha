The Directorate, People Resourcing and Compliance has responsibility for:

*   Recruitment
*   Discipline
*   Compliance
*   Administration of:
    *   Applications for pensions
    *   Leave matters (other than training and study leave)
*   People Support and Wellness
*   The Services Commissions
