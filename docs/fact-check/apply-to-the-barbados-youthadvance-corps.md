# Fact-check: Apply to the Barbados YouthADVANCE Corps

- **Live page:** <https://alpha.gov.bb/work-employment/apply-to-the-barbados-youthadvance-corps>
- **Source file:** `src/content/apply-to-the-barbados-youthadvance-corps.md`
- **Last checked:** 2026-05-28
- **Summary:** 17 claims reviewed — 7 verified, 4 discrepant, 6 unverifiable. Average certainty: **68%**.

---

## Headline issues for triage

1. **Ministry name is wrong throughout the page.** Lines 29 and 84 use "Ministry of Youth and Community Empowerment." The correct current name is "Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment," confirmed in `src/data/ministries.ts`, on `youthaffairs.gov.bb`, `byac.gov.bb`, and `gisbarbados.gov.bb`. Both occurrences need correcting.

2. **Residential programme duration is contradicted by multiple authoritative sources.** Line 101 states "a 6-week residential programme." The Division of Youth Affairs programme page and the BYAC's own programme page both state "Residential Training (10 weeks)." A December 2022 Barbados Today article adds that the residential component comprises a "six-week pre-residential preparatory programme" followed by "nine weeks" at Barbados Defence Force Paragon Base — suggesting the total residential involvement may be approximately 15 weeks. The page's "6-week" figure is the lowest and least-supported version and is a high-stakes claim for families assessing whether their child can commit to the programme.

3. **"National Insurance Scheme" is an outdated name.** Line 49 describes the application form as asking about "your National Insurance Scheme." The NIS was formally renamed the National Insurance and Social Security Service (NISSS) on 1 December 2023. The old name has been superseded for over two years.

4. **The $600/month stipend figure has not been confirmed in any current Tier 1 source.** The only publicly documented source for this figure is a 2019 Barbados Today article — before the programme launched. No current official page (youthaffairs.gov.bb, byac.gov.bb, gov.bb) publishes the current stipend amount. The figure may have changed in the intervening seven years.

5. **"Apply all year round" is unconfirmed.** Official BYAC sources reference specific cohort intake dates, not a continuous rolling-admissions model. If applications are intake-based, the "all year round" claim may give applicants incorrect expectations.

  - Source: [byac.gov.bb — Recruitment](http://byac.gov.bb/recruitment/)
