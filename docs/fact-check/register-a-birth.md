# Fact-check: Register a birth

- **Live page:** <https://alpha.gov.bb/family-birth-relationships/register-a-birth>
- **Source file:** `src/content/register-a-birth/index.md`
- **Last checked:** 2026-05-27
- **Summary:** 15 claims reviewed — 11 verified, 1 discrepant (typo), 3 unverifiable. Average certainty: **84%**.

---

## Headline issues for triage

1. **Typo "Cane Carden" → "Cane Garden"** (line 120) — already flagged as F-003.
2. **"BDS $20 late registration fee" is unverified.** The Barbados Judicial System and Registration Department state "a late fee is required" but don't publish the amount. Verify with the Registrar's office before publishing the specific figure.
3. **Senior-citizen $1 rate is missing.** The page mentions BDS $5 per certified copy (line 23) but omits the $1 rate for people aged 60+. Cross-page consistency with `get-birth-certificate/start.md` should be maintained.
4. **District B and District C are flagged as temporary relocations** ("currently located at…") — needs an "as of" date so citizens know how fresh the info is.
