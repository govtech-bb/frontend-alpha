## Headline issues for triage

1. **Speightstown welfare office building name may be wrong.** Site says "Republic Bank Building, Chapel Street, Speightstown". Welfare Department's own listings refer to the "BNB Building" in Speightstown. BNB (Barbados National Bank) was acquired by Republic Bank in 2010, so the building may have been rebranded — but verify which name is current.
2. **Welfare Department phone format unparseable.** Site says `+1 246-535-1000/16/23`. Official Welfare Department contact lists `1 (246) 535-1000` and `1 (246) 535-1023`. There is no `/16/` extension on the published list — the "16" is unverified.
3. **"Country Road" address is missing context.** Welfare publishes this as "Country Road, St. Michael, **opposite the National Housing Corporation**" — that landmark is genuinely useful and missing from the site.
4. **Typo "assitance"** in line 33 heading (already flagged as F-004).
