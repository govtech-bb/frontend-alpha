# Fact-check: Apply for financial assistance

- **Live page:** <https://alpha.gov.bb/money-financial-support/apply-financial-assistance>
- **Source file:** `src/content/apply-financial-assistance.md`
- **Last checked:** 2026-05-28
- **Summary:** 18 claims reviewed — 9 verified, 4 discrepant, 5 unverifiable. Average certainty: **76%**.

---

## Headline issues for triage

1. **"Electronic transfer of funds is not available yet" is false.** The Welfare Department's direct deposit / EFT system for National Assistance Grants went live on 21 May 2024 (GIS press release). The page still tells citizens EFT is pending. This is the highest-priority correction: citizens may avoid providing bank details unnecessarily, and the government may receive fewer enrolments than expected.

2. **Speightstown office building name is wrong.** The page says "Republic Bank Building, Chapel St." — every government and GIS source from 2017 onwards calls it the "BNB Building" in Speightstown. BNB (Barbados National Bank) was acquired by Republic Bank in 2010 but the welfare office building retains the BNB name in all official listings. Update to "BNB Building, Chapel Street, Speightstown, Saint Peter".

3. **Phone number format `+1 246-535-1000/16/23` is non-standard and partially wrong.** The two published numbers are `(246) 535-1000` and `(246) 535-1023`. The notation `/16/` appears nowhere in any official source — "16" is not a recognisable extension format. The GIS May 2024 direct deposit press release also lists a third number: `535-1005`. Rewrite as `(246) 535-1000`, `(246) 535-1005`, `(246) 535-1023`.

4. **"Weymouth Corporate Center" vs "Centre" — inconsistent across the page.** gov.bb uses American spelling "Center"; GIS consistently uses British spelling "Centre". The page uses both spellings (l.42 "Center", l.98 "Centre"). Pick one and be consistent. GIS/official government communications favour "Centre".
