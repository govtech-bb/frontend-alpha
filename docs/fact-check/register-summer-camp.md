## Headline issues for triage

1. **"Registration opens in May 2026" is operational and time-critical.** Today is 2026-05-27. The page is either about to be stale (if registration just opened) or already wrong. Check with Ministry of Youth and update the live status.
2. **"Around 45 summer camps" is close to actual.** Ministry of Youth confirms the 2024 programme ran at **46 locations**. "Around 45" is fine as a rolling estimate, but worth confirming the 2026 count.
3. **Contact details are missing from the page.** Ministry of Youth publishes phone `(246) 535-3835`, email `YDP@barbados.gov.bb`, and a physical address (Sky Mall, Haggatt Hall, St. Michael). None of these appear on the content page — useful additions for citizens who can't register online.
