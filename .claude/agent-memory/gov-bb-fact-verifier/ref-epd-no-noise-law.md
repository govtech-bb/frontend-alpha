Per EPD — Noise Pollution Control (https://www.epd.gov.bb/What-We-Do/Noise-Pollution-Control/):

"There is no noise legislation in Barbados."

The EPD uses WHO Community Noise Guidelines as reference standards (e.g. 55 dBA daytime, 45 dBA nighttime in residential areas).

Impact for alpha.gov.bb: The loud-music-permit page's definition of loud music ("music you can hear outside the boundary of a property, or at a time when people would reasonably expect quiet") has no statutory basis. Content team should note this is plain-language guidance, not a legal definition.
