## Key verified facts

- **Grantley Adams International Airport (GAIA)**
  - Location: Seawell, Christ Church parish — south-eastern part of island, ~13 km southeast of Bridgetown
  - Highway: Adams-Barrow-Cummins (ABC) Highway (also Highway 7)
  - Description confirmed by gov.bb: "Barbados's only airport and the aviation hub of the Eastern Caribbean"
  - Contact: (246) 536-1300 (main); 536-1302 (reception); 536-1336 (duty manager)
  - Official site: gaia.bb; gov.bb state body page: gov.bb/owa/State-Bodies/grantley-adams-international
  - Travel time to west coast: ~30 minutes confirmed by multiple sources

- **Bridgetown Port / Barbados Port Inc.**
  - Official name: Barbados Port Inc. (BPI); facility name: Port of Bridgetown
  - Address: Cube Blue, Prescod Boulevard, Bridgetown, St. Michael, BB 11142
  - Phone: 1 (246) 434-6100
  - Official site: barbadosport.com (HTTPS)
  - "BTA" is the obsolete name for the tourism body that organises quayside entertainment — see below

- **Port Saint Charles**
  - Location: Heywoods, parish of Saint Peter, **northwest coast** (NOT "western coast")
  - Proximity: ~1 km north of Speightstown
  - Marina admeasurement: 1549 m² (marina water area)
  - Inland lagoon depth at low tide: 14 feet
  - Community size: 22 acres
  - Bedrooms: 1 to **6** bedrooms (NOT 5) — official site lists 6-bedroom units
  - Official site: portstcharles.com (HTTPS)
  - Officially designated port of entry for private yachts (customs/immigration clearance dockside)

- **BTA → BTMI/BTPA name change**
  - The Barbados Tourism Authority (BTA) was restructured in 2014 into:
    - Barbados Tourism Marketing Inc. (BTMI) — marketing, tourism product/experience
    - Barbados Tourism Product Authority (BTPA) — enforcement of Accommodation Services Act
  - "BTA" is now obsolete; alpha.gov.bb ports-of-entry.md incorrectly uses "BTA" (F-063 filed)
  - BTMI website: corporate.visitbarbados.org
  - tourism.gov.bb lists agencies: gov.bb/About/Agencies/
