## Ministry of Energy and Business Development (MEBD)

**Official names in use (conflicting):**
- gov.bb and alpha.gov.bb use: "Ministry of Energy and Business Development"
- Barbados Parliament cabinet list (Feb 2026) and Barbados Today use the portfolio title: "Energy, Business Development and Commerce"
- energy.gov.bb says: "Ministry of Energy and Business"
- commerce.gov.bb is also operated by this ministry (TLS cert error as of 2026-05-28)
- The legal/gazetted name is unconfirmed; needs Cabinet Secretariat confirmation
