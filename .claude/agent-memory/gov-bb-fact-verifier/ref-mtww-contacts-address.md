## Official name
**Ministry of Transport, Works and Water Resources (MTWW)** — confirmed on gov.bb canonical ministry listing.
- Older names "Ministry of Transport and Works" and "Ministry of Transport & Works" still appear on the ministry's own website branding (mtw.gov.bb) but are not the official current name.
- alpha.gov.bb conductor licence page uses the outdated "Ministry of Transport and Works" — flagged as discrepant (Confidence 90%).

## Addresses
- **Permanent/Main HQ:** Pine East–West Boulevard, The Pine, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I.

## Conductor licence mandatory training (new, Nov 2024)
- Transport Authority Regulations 2024 require all PSV conductors to complete a mandatory certification course at Barbados Community College (cost: BBD $200), with recertification every two years.
- This is a parallel/prerequisite requirement separate from the BLA conductor licence — not yet reflected on alpha.gov.bb conductor licence page.
- Source: [Barbados Today — Nov 2024](https://barbadostoday.bb/2024/11/13/psv-operators-warned-mandatory-certification-tied-to-jobs/)
