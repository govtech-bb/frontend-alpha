## Barbados Revenue Authority (BRA) — Weymouth address — confirmed 2026-05-28

**Canonical HQ address:** Weymouth Corporate Centre, Roebuck Street, St. Michael (confirmed on bra.gov.bb, gov.bb, GIS).

**WRONG road name to avoid:** "Spring Garden Highway" — this is NOT where Weymouth Corporate Centre is located. Spring Garden Highway is approximately 4 km away in a different direction. Any content with "BRA, Weymouth Corporate Centre, Spring Garden Highway" is discrepant.

**Public Entertainment Unit direct contacts:**
- Phone: (246) 232-2045 (confirmed via BRA Public Entertainment portal search results)
- Email: publicentertainment@bra.gov.bb (confirmed on bra.gov.bb)
- General BRA enquiries: (246) 429-ETAX / 429-3829; bramail@bra.gov.bb

**Place of Public Entertainment licence fee range:** $200–$5,000 depending on venue classification (confirmed on bra.gov.bb). Full fee schedule only inside the portal.

**BRA Public Entertainment Portal TLS issue:** publicentertainment.bra.gov.bb has an ongoing TLS cert error (confirmed 2026-05-27 and 2026-05-28). See [[ref-bra-public-entertainment-tls]].
