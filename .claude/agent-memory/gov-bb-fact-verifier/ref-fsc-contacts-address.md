## Financial Services Commission (FSC)

- **Official name:** Financial Services Commission
- **Address (current):** Bay Corporate Building, Bay Street, St. Michael, BB14038 — moved here December 2020 per GIS announcement
- **Phone:** +1 (246) 421-2142 (one voice line only)
- **Fax:** (246) 421-2146 — this is the FAX, NOT a second phone line. gov.bb lists both without labelling; fsc.gov.bb Contact Us only lists the phone.
- **Email (general):** info@fsc.gov.bb
- **Sector emails:** creditunions@, insurance@, pensions@, securities@, tcr@, newapplications@, registration.licensing@ — all @fsc.gov.bb
- **Website:** https://www.fsc.gov.bb/ (HTTPS — the alpha.gov.bb page uses http:// which is incorrect)
- **Established:** 1 April 2011 under Financial Services Commission Act, 2010-21

## Acts under FSC supervision

1. Exempt Insurance Act, Cap. 308A
2. Insurance Act, Cap. 310
3. Occupational Pension Benefits Act, Cap. 350B
4. Securities Act, Cap. 318A
5. Mutual Funds Act, Cap. 320B
6. Co-operative Societies Act, Cap. 378A (credit unions — NOTE: "Co-operative" singular, NOT "Co-operatives" plural)

## Key discrepancy found on alpha.gov.bb (2026-05-28)

- `(246) 421-2146` is labelled as a second phone number — it is actually the fax. Triage card F-037.
- "Co-operatives Societies Act" on the page is misspelled — should be "Co-operative Societies Act". Triage card F-038.
- Website URL uses `http://` — should be `https://`.
