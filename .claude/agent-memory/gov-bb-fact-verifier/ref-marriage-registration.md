## Registration Department address for marriage registration

**Correct address (2019–present):** Level 1, Supreme Court Complex, Whitepark Road, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I.

**WRONG address (still on alpha.gov.bb register-a-marriage line 11):** "Registration Department, Coleridge Street, Bridgetown"

"Coleridge Street" is where the Henry Forde and David Simmons Judicial Complex is — courts relocated there from 2023. The Registration Department itself moved back to Whitepark Road in 2019 and has been there since. This error is present in the gov.bb/Citizens/register-marriage source page too — both copied from the same stale original.

**Authoritative sources:**
- https://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/useful-links/for-public/services/vital-statistics-registration/getting-married-and-registration-of-marriages — "Level 1, Supreme Court Complex, Whitepark Road, Bridgetown"
- https://www.gov.bb/Departments/registration — "Supreme Court Complex, Whitepark Road, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I."
- https://www.gov.bb/news_article.php?id=28 — 2019 news article confirming relocation back to Supreme Court Complex, Whitepark Road

## Registration deadline

"A marriage must be registered within the first ten (10) days of every month." — per barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb. This deadline is NOT on alpha.gov.bb. It binds the Marriage Officer or Magistrate who performed the ceremony.

## Certificate fees

- Nationals/citizens/residents: BDS $10 per copy
- Non-Nationals/non-citizens: BDS $20 per copy
- Apostille (when required by a foreign government): BDS $50 additional

Sources: https://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/useful-links/for-public/how-to-order/certificates and https://www.gov.bb/Citizens/marriage-fees

## Phone

1 (246) 535-9700 — same as all Registration Dept / Barbados Judicial System contacts. Confirmed on three independent Tier 1 sources.

## Email / fax

registrarsupremecourt@barbados.gov.bb; fax 1-246-426-2405 — per barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb (not on the alpha.gov.bb page).

## Governing legislation

Marriage Act, Cap. 218A; Marriage (Forms & Fees) Regulations, S.I. 58/1979. Both administered by the Records Branch. PDF is binary and not machine-readable from the public web.

## Same-sex marriage

Legally prohibited in Barbados under the 1979 Marriage Act (marriage defined as between a man and a woman). Gendered "husband/wife" terminology on the page accurately reflects current law.

## barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb URL pattern

The marriage-registration-specific page is at:

**How to apply:** When any alpha.gov.bb page references "the Registration Department" or "register a marriage/death/birth" check the address against the Whitepark Road canonical. Flag any "Coleridge Street" occurrence as a Tier A discrepancy.
