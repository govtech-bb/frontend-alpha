## Ministry of Educational Transformation

- **Canonical name:** "Ministry of Educational Transformation" (MEDT) — per gov.bb/Ministries/education and src/data/ministries.ts line 234
- **Abbreviation variants:** "MEDT" (gov.bb), "MEdT" (education.gov.bb About page, which also uses "Ministry of Education Transformation" — inconsistent with gov.bb)
- **Official website:** mes.gov.bb (per gov.bb directory) — but this 301 redirects to education.gov.bb
- **Live domain:** education.gov.bb (as of 2026-05-28)
- **Ministry split:** In February 2025, the former "Ministry of Education, Technological and Vocational Training" was split into:
  1. Ministry of Educational Transformation (Chad Blackman)
  2. Ministry of Training and Tertiary Education (Sandra Husbands)
- **Address:** Elsie Payne Complex, Constitution Road, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I.
- **Phone:** (246) 535-0600 (PBX); Permanent Secretary direct: (246) 535-0607; Secretary to PS: (246) 535-0608
- **Email:** info@mes.gov.bb; ps@mes.gov.bb; teachervacancy@mes.gov.bb (for teacher vacancy queries)
- **Fax:** (246) 436-2411

## Current Minister (as of 2026-02-16)
- **Chad Blackman, M.P.** — Minister of Educational Transformation
- Reappointed 16 February 2026 after winning St James North by-election
- Previously served as Senator from February 2025 appointment; now elected MP
- Source: [Barbados Today — Cabinet ministers sworn in (2026-02-16)](https://barbadostoday.bb/2026/02/16/cabinet-ministers-sworn-in/); [Barbados Today 2026-05-21](https://barbadostoday.bb/2026/05-21/minister-blackmans-message-for-teachers-professional-day/)
