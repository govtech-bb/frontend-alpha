The official canonical name, as published on gov.bb/Ministries and the ministry's own page, is:

**Ministry of Industry, Innovation, Science and Technology (MIST)**

- Address: 3rd and 4th Floor, Baobab Tower, Warrens, St. Michael
- Phone: (246) 535-1200 / 1201 / 1202 (PBX range; 535-1201 is the primary directory number)
- Fax: (246) 535-1284
- Email: psmist@barbados.gov.bb
- Permanent Secretary direct: (246) 535-1211; PS Secretary: (246) 535-1238
- URL: https://www.gov.bb/Ministries/innovation-science-smart-technology (slug is a stale artefact from the pre-2021 name but still resolves)

**Minister (Feb 2026):** Senator The Hon. Jonathan W. D. Reid
- Canonical portfolio title: **"Minister of Industry, Innovation, Science and Technology"** (gov.bb, GIS tag, GIS Facebook, Barbados Today budget coverage)
- Reversed variant "Minister of Innovation, Industry, Science and Technology" used in: Barbados Today swearing-in article (16 Feb 2026), CBC News, barbadosparliament.com
- ministries.ts line 727 uses the reversed order — **DISCREPANT (F-096)**

**Keywords/abbreviation:** Official acronym is "MIST" (4 letters). ministries.ts line 719 uses "MIIST" (5 letters, double-I) — **DISCREPANT (F-097 range)**

**Common wrong ministry name variants found on alpha.gov.bb:**
- "Ministry of Innovation, Science and Smart Technology" — missing "Industry", "Smart" not in official name (terms-conditions.md line 29, F-090)
- Role title word-order inversion in ministries.ts (F-096)

**GovTech Barbados Ltd.** (govtech.bb) is a separate government-owned company (incorporated 18 Sep 2023) that operates alpha.gov.bb under MIST's portfolio.

**Data Protection Commission** is under MIST: 5th Floor, SSA Building, Vaucluse, St. Thomas; Tel: 1 (246) 536-1200 / 536-1212.

**BNSI** (Barbados National Standards Institution) and **Analytical Services** are also confirmed under MIST but are absent from ministries.ts associatedDepartments.

**Future Barbados** moved from PMO to MIST as innovation arm (March 2026).
