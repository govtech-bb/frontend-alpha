Per gov.bb — Police Department (https://www.gov.bb/Departments/police-department):

"The headquarters for the RBPF is in the former Barclays Bank Complex on Lower Roebuck Street, Bridgetown, Saint Michael."

Certainty: 90%.

The alpha.gov.bb loud-music-permit page refers to "Barbados Police Service headquarters in Bridgetown" but does not give the street address. Adding "Lower Roebuck Street" would help citizens making postal or in-person enquiries.
