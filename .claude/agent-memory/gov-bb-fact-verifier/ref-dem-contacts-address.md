## Department of Emergency Management (DEM)

**Address:** The George Greaves Building, #24 Warrens Industrial Park, Warrens, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I.

**Phone:** (246) 438-7575
**Fax:** (246) 421-8612
**Email:** deminfo@barbados.gov.bb
**Social media:** @DEM246 (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube)

**District Emergency Organisations (DEOs):**
- 30 DEOs distributed across Barbados
- Function as the volunteer arm of DEM
- Mandate: damage assessment, needs analysis, and relief distribution
- Provide forum for community education and training on hurricane, flood, earthquake, and fire preparedness
- DEM contact for local DEO queries: 438-7575

**Mandate:** "develop, promote and maintain a comprehensive National Disaster Management Programme" covering Prevention/Mitigation, Preparedness, Response, Recovery across all hazards.
