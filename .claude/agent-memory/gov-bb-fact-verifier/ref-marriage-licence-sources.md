## Marriage licence — verified facts (2026-05-28)

### Fees (all verified, 95%)
- Licence fee: BDS$200 cash (both nationals and non-nationals)
- Stamp: $10 nationals / $25 non-nationals (obtainable at Ministry)
- Special arrangements (weekends/holidays/home visit): BDS$100 or USD$50
- Magistrate court ceremony: nationals $100 (in court) / $125 (outside); non-nationals $250 / $350
- Marriage certificate: nationals $10 / non-nationals $20
- Apostille: $50

### Legal references
- Marriage Act CAP 218A governs; s.4 — under-16 void; s.26 — minor consent (Schedule 2)
- Act uses gender-neutral "two persons" language in most sections; s.3 references "man and woman" only in void-marriage prohibited-degree context
- "Male and female" on page and on gov.bb source page: reflects administrative practice; legal basis contested post-2022 Sexual Offences Act ruling (s.9 and s.12 struck down Dec 2022); GovBB team should get OAG opinion before publishing
- Licence validity: 3 months (confirmed)
