## Police Certificate of Character

- **Fee:** BBD $20 (confirmed 2026-05-28)
- **Application URL:** https://forms.gov.bb/CertificateOfCharacter (live; confirmed)
- **Bulk upload URL:** https://forms.gov.bb/Bulk_CertificateOfCharacter
- **Payment:** EZPAY+ (credit card instant; direct debit 5 business days; mMoney instant) OR cash at any Post Office island-wide
- **Process:** Apply online at forms.gov.bb; certificate and copy of application sent to the email address provided
- **Required info:** active email, national registration number (if applicable), personal details, residential address history, nationality, mother's name, phone number
