The National Employment Bureau (NEB) was renamed the Barbados Employment and Career Counselling Service (BECCS) effective 1 April (year ~2018; announcement May 2017 per Loop Barbados). GIS article title "Name Change For Employment Bureau April 1st" confirmed by search index — direct URL https://gisbarbados.gov.bb/blog/name-change-for-employment-bureau-april-1st/ returns HTTP 403.

All labour.gov.bb employment service pages (One Stop Resource Centre, Employment Services listing, Guidance and Counseling) now reference BECCS exclusively; "National Employment Bureau" does not appear on any current Tier 1 page.

The alpha.gov.bb Jobseekers page (`src/content/jobseekers.md` line 13) still uses "National Employment Bureau" — flagged as F-048 (Tier B).

BECCS is described as "the Government of Barbados' national employment service" on labour.gov.bb/employment-services/.
