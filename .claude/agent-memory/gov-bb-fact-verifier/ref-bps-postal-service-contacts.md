## Change of address / mail redirection
- Domestic fee: BDS$13.00
- Business fee: BDS$30.00
- Business requirements: business name in CAPITALS + company stamp (with signature) in the applicant field
- No mention of Certificate of Incorporation, National ID card, or business registration number in any BPS-published source
- Forms cannot be backdated — date must be day of submission or a future date
- Duration: NOT stated on bps.gov.bb — alpha.gov.bb claims 6 months (unverified)
- Form PDF: https://www.gov.bb/media_files/PostOffice_RedirNotice.pdf (binary, confirmed live 2026-05-28, not machine-readable)
- Service page: https://bps.gov.bb/change-of-address/

## Deceased mail redirection (post-office-redirection-deceased)
- No separate fee category published for deceased-estate redirections; BDS$13 domestic rate is best inference but unconfirmed for this service
- "Lasting power of attorney" in start.md is legally wrong: (a) PoA terminates at death; (b) Barbados has no "lasting" PoA in statute (ordinary PoA only, which is also revoked at death/incapacity, per STEP country assessment). Correct docs: Letters Testamentary (will) or Letters of Administration (no will), from Supreme Court Probate Unit — F-066 filed
- 6-month duration also unverified for deceased category — F-067 filed
- BDS$13 fee for deceased category unverified — F-067 filed
- Barbados has NO "Lasting Power of Attorney" legislation: ordinary PoA only, automatically void at death/incapacity (confirmed by STEP: https://www.step.org/mental-capacity/public/what-power-attorney-or-power-representation-can-i-get-one-where-i-live)
- Letters Testamentary = grant of Probate; issued by Supreme Court Probate Unit (Level 3, Supreme Court Complex, Whitepark Road)
- Letters of Administration issued for intestate estates — also from Supreme Court Probate Unit
