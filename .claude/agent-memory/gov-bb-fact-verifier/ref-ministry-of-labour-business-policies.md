## Associated bodies — NISSS naming alert

- **"National Insurance Department" is STALE.** That body was transformed into the **National Insurance and Social Security Service (NISSS)** on 1 December 2023 via the National Insurance and Social Security (Amendment) (No.1 and No.2) Acts, 2023. Gov.bb's /Departments/national-insurance page has not been updated but the NISSS's own website (nis.gov.bb) and the nis.gov.bb/national-insurance-and-social-security-service-on-day-one/ announcement are definitive. All references to "National Insurance Department" in ministries.ts and content pages should be updated to "National Insurance and Social Security Service (NISSS)".
- **BECCS not in associatedDepartments.** The Barbados Employment and Career Counselling Service (BECCS) operates from 1st Floor East of the same complex (phone 535-1535) and is a key ministry body. It does not appear in ministries.ts associatedDepartments.
- Sources: [nis.gov.bb — NISSS Day One](https://www.nis.gov.bb/national-insurance-and-social-security-service-on-day-one/); [nis.gov.bb — About Us](https://www.nis.gov.bb/about-us/)

## Mission statement (verified verbatim 2026-05-28)

"To assist the Government and its Social Partners in promoting opportunities for the provision of decent and productive work, in conditions of freedom of association, equity, security and human dignity and to provide quality social and economic benefits for Barbadians."

Source: [labour.gov.bb — About Us](https://labour.gov.bb/policies-programmes/about-us/)

## Vision / workforce development statement (verified verbatim 2026-05-28)

"To develop a Barbadian workforce, including persons with disabilities, that is well informed, properly trained and conditioned for the local, regional and extra-regional labour markets."

Source: [labour.gov.bb — About Us](https://labour.gov.bb/policies-programmes/about-us/)

## Labour legislation list

The Ministry publishes 25 Acts at [labour.gov.bb/labour-legislation](https://labour.gov.bb/labour-legislation). Key acts include:
- Severance Payments Act CAP 355A
- Employment Rights Act 2012
- Safety and Health at Work Act CAP 356
- Trade Unions Act CAP 361
- Employment (Prevention of Discrimination) Act
- Employment Sexual Harassment (Prevention) Act 2017
- Labour Holidays with Pay Act 2017

None of these Acts are named on the alpha.gov.bb "Business policies and law" page.

See [[ref-bla-contacts-and-legislation]] for the Road Traffic Act (CAP 295) which governs driving — separate from labour legislation.
