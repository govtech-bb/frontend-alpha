## Barbados Fire Service (BFS) — confirmed 2026-05-28

**Current headquarters (from October 2025):** Cadogan, Mayers, Marshall (CMM) Emergency Services Complex, Prince Road, Pine Plantation Road, St. Michael.

**Old address (stale — do not use):** Level 5, General Post Office Building, Cheapside, St. Michael. This address still appears on gov.bb/Departments/fire-service as of 2026-05-28 — that page is outdated.

**Fire Prevention & Code Enforcement Unit:** Now at CMM Complex; phone (246) 535-7829 (confirmed on both old and new sources — number unchanged).

**Headquarters PBX:** 535-7824/25

**Correct website:** https://fireservice.gov.bb (canonical, confirmed). https://bfs.gov.bb may redirect here but was not independently confirmed.

**Fees schedule:** https://fireservice.gov.bb/fees-schedule/ (published online; specific amounts not on open web — need portal or direct contact)

**Move date:** October 2025 (per Barbados Today 2025-10-28)
