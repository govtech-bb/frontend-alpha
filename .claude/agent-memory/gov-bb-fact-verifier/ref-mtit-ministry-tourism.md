## Ministry of Tourism and International Transport (MTIT / MTI)

**Official abbreviation:** MTI (per gov.bb page title) — NOT "MTIT" (no Tier 1 source uses MTIT)

**Official website:** tourism.gov.bb

**Minister:** The Hon. G. P. Ian Gooding-Edghill, J.P., M.P. (St Michael West Central). Both J.P. and M.P. post-nominals confirmed by barbadosparliament.com Cabinet Ministers page. ## Civil Aviation

"Barbados Civil Aviation Department (BCAD)" — STALE. Parliament established the **Barbados Civil Aviation Authority (BCAA)** on 25 October 2022 under Civil Aviation Act 2022-19. BCAD is superseded.

BCAA address: Civil Aviation Authority Building, Charnocks, Christ Church, Barbados BB17087
BCAA phone: (246) 535-0001 | Fax: (246) 535-0028 | Email: civil.aviation@bcaa.gov.bb
Website: bcaa.gov.bb

## Tourism agencies

**BTA (Barbados Tourism Authority)** — OBSOLETE since 2014. Replaced by:
- Barbados Tourism Marketing Inc. (BTMI) — marketing body; corporate.visitbarbados.org; One Barbados Place, Warrens; (246) 535-3700
- Barbados Tourism Product Authority (BTPA) — accommodation licensing & product standards regulator

**BTMI full agency list (tourism.gov.bb/About/Agencies):** 13+ entities including BAASEC, BCAA, BCSL, BPI, BTMI, BTPA, CAH, CIAL, CBL, GAIA, Hotels & Resorts Ltd, NPHL, NPDI

**BCSL abbreviation:** "BCSL" is correct (gov.bb, tourism.gov.bb page title); the URL slug on tourism.gov.bb uses "BSCL" (transposition error in URL, not in content).

BCSL address: Lloyd Erskine Sandiford Centre, Two Mile Hill, St. Michael. Phone: (246) 467-8200 / (246) 431-9795. Website: lescbarbados.com
