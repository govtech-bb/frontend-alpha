## Key facts verified 2026-05-28

**Ministry:** Ministry of Labour, Social Security and Third Sector  
**Canonical address:** 3rd Floor West Wing, Warrens Office Complex, Warrens, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I.  
— Note: content page used "3rd Floor West" (missing "Wing") and "Warrens Close" (non-existent street — should be just "Warrens")  
**Ministry phone:** (246) 535-1400 (Planning & Admin); (246) 535-1535 (BECCS — 1st Floor East)  
**Programme email:** jobstartplus@labour.gov.bb

**Eligibility:**  
- 16–24 years old (general)  
- 16–34 years old (persons with disabilities)  
- Not currently employed or enrolled in a training institution  

**Training duration:** 3 weeks (expanded from 2 weeks per programme manager Erika Watson, confirmed March 2026)  
— labour.gov.bb/jobstartplus/ still says "two-week" as of April 2025 — not yet updated  

**Training hours:** NOT publicly specified in any source (page claims 8:45am–4:30pm, 5 days/week — unverified; 2022 article said "half-day sessions")  

**Registration model:** Cohort-based with hard deadlines (e.g., April 11, 2025 for May 2025 cohort); NOT year-round open as alpha.gov.bb states  

**Curriculum (verified):** Effective Communication, Teamwork, Conflict Resolution, Anger Management, Time Management, Work Ethics, Resume Writing, Effective Job Search Strategies, Interview Techniques — all 9 confirmed on labour.gov.bb  

**Stipend:** Confirmed to exist; covers "training expenses" per March 2026 article; "lunch and travel" wording on alpha.gov.bb not directly corroborated  
— Disability cohort stipend: $150/week (increased to $250 by Cabinet decision)  

**Placement:** Minimum 3 months, maximum 1 year work experience  
**Stats (Aug 2025):** ~800 trained, 372 placements since 2019 launch  

**Official URL:** https://labour.gov.bb/jobstartplus/ — LIVE as of 2026-05-28  
**GIS article URL:** https://gisbarbados.gov.bb/blog/youth-urged-to-sign-up-for-training-under-job-start-plus-programme/ — returns 403
