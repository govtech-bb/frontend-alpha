## National Sports Council — key facts (verified 2026-05-28)

**Current official address (Tier 1):**
The National Sports Complex, Wildey Gymnasium, Garfield Sobers Sports Complex, Wildey, St. Michael BB 22026
- Source: gov.bb/Departments/sports-council AND src/data/departments.ts (slug: sports-council)

**Former address (historic, now outdated):**
Blenheim, My Lord's Hill, St. Michael — this is what alpha.gov.bb still says (DISCREPANT). Carib Guide review (~2020) confirms NSC relocated to Wildey. However, NSC 2026 camp materials reference a "Blenheim Location" for form drop-off alongside Garfield Sobers Gymnasium, suggesting Blenheim may still be an active satellite point. Do not silently drop My Lords Hill without NSC confirmation.

**Contacts:**
- Phone: (246) 535-9601 / (246) 535-9602
- Fax: (246) 535-9659
- Email: nsc.bdos@barbados.gov.bb
- Website: http://www.nsc.gov.bb

**Facility name:**
- Full official name: "Sir Garfield Sobers Sports Complex" (gov.bb State Bodies page)
- Short form used by gov.bb: "Garfield Sobers Sports Complex"
- The NSC building: "Wildey Gymnasium" (also called "Garfield Sobers Gymnasium" in GIS)
- alpha.gov.bb uses "Garfield Sobers Complex" — drops "Sports" and "Sir" (minor discrepancy)

**2026 summer camp:**
- NSC Summer Camp 2026 Coach Assistant Applications are open (confirmed nsc.gov.bb May 2026)
- "ALL completed forms must be returned to the Garfield Sobers Gymnasium"
- NSC also refers to a "Blenheim Location" for form submission
- 2025 camp ran July 14 – August 22, 2025 (39th year); 1,574 participants; 23 sports
- Application form is a downloadable PDF from nsc.gov.bb

**Volunteer eligibility age:**
- alpha.gov.bb claims 19+; no public NSC source confirms this floor
- Division of Youth Affairs (different programme: National Summer Camp) uses 16+
- "19+" is plausible but unverifiable; requires NSC confirmation

**Recruitment/training timing:**
- "Recruitment begins around April" — NOT corroborated; 2025 Division of Youth Affairs opened late June; NSC 2026 also opened around May/June
- "Training in June" — inconsistent with late-June recruitment pattern; likely July or later
- "Mid-June notification" — plausible if recruitment opens May, but unverified

**Police Certificate of Character (BBD $20):**
- Confirmed live: forms.gov.bb/CertificateOfCharacter
- Fee: BBD $20; valid 6 months; 24–48 hr turnaround after payment
- See also [[ref-police-certificate-of-character]]

**MYSCE is the parent ministry:**
- Full name: "Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment"
- NSC is an associated department under the Sports category
