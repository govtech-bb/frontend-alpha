**Visa application (from July 2025):**
The Barbados Entry Visa process is now FULLY ONLINE at apps.immigration.gov.bb. Processing: 3 weeks. Fees: US$107 single entry (valid 3 months); US$211 multiple entry (valid 6 months). One photograph required (not two). Paper form in duplicate is obsolete.

**CARICOM entry (from Oct 2025):**
CARICOM nationals (except Haiti) visa-exempt, up to 6 months/year. From 1 Oct 2025: Barbados, Belize, Dominica, and St. Vincent nationals have full free movement (indefinite stay).
