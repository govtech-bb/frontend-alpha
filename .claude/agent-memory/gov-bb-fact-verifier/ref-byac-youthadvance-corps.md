## Barbados YouthADVANCE Corps (BYAC) — verified facts (2026-05-28)

**Authoritative sources:**
- byac.gov.bb (dedicated BYAC portal)
- youthaffairs.gov.bb/about-the-youth-advance-corps/
- youthaffairs.gov.bb/programme-channels/barbados-youthadvance-corps-2/
- gov.bb/State-Bodies/barbados-youth-advance-corps

**Address:** No. 33 Warrens Industrial Park, Warrens, St. Michael (verified 99%)

**Phone:** (246) 535-0180 / (246) 535-0184

**Email:** BYAC@barbados.gov.bb

**Age eligibility:** 16 to 20 years old (verified 99%; 2019 pre-launch article said 16–23 but all post-launch sources say 16–20)

**Programme duration:** 2 years (verified 99%)

**Residential component (DISCREPANT):**
- byac.gov.bb/barbados-youth-advance-corps-accepting-applications/ says "six-week residential programme"
- youthaffairs.gov.bb/about-the-youth-advance-corps/ and byac.gov.bb/programme/ both say "Residential Training (10 weeks)"
- Barbados Today (Dec 2022) reports "six-week pre-residential preparatory programme" + "nine weeks at Barbados Defence Force Paragon Base" (Christ Church) — two-phase structure totalling ~15 weeks
- The "6-week" figure on alpha.gov.bb is the least-supported version; confidence it's wrong: 80%

**Stipend:** $600/month mentioned in 2019 Barbados Today article only; NOT confirmed on any current Tier 1 source (byac.gov.bb, youthaffairs.gov.bb, gov.bb). Treat as unverifiable until agency confirms.

**Ministry name:** Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment (MYSCE) — NOT "Ministry of Youth and Community Empowerment" (old name used on alpha.gov.bb page at lines 29 and 84)

**Online application:** byac.gov.bb/recruitment/ — form available online (not just paper)

**Applications:** Official sources reference specific intake cohorts (e.g. September 2021, January 2022); "all year round" claim on alpha.gov.bb is not confirmed in any Tier 1 source.

**NIS name change (relevant to application form description):**
- "National Insurance Scheme" was renamed "National Insurance and Social Security Service (NISSS)" on 1 December 2023
- Source: nis.gov.bb/about-us/ and nis.gov.bb/national-insurance-and-social-security-service-on-day-one/

**Qualifications:** CSEC, City & Guilds, certificates via SJPI, BCC, BIMAP, BVTB — confirmed by byac.gov.bb/training/ and search snippets

**Year 2 location of residential:** Barbados Defence Force Paragon Base, Christ Church

**GIS coverage:** Multiple GIS articles on BYAC return HTTP 403 on WebFetch; use web search to verify via snippets.
