## National Conservation Commission (NCC) — confirmed 2026-05-28

**Canonical address:** NCC, Codrington House, Waterford, St. Michael (also cited simply as "Codrington House, St. Michael"). Full: Codrington Road, Waterford, Saint Michael, BB11042. "Haggatt Hall" is NOT correct — the two areas are ~3 km apart.

**Phones:** (246) 536-0617 (main), (246) 536-0600 (PBX), (246) 536-0681 (fax)

**Emails:** ncc@ncc.gov.bb; specialprojectsoffice@ncc.gov.bb

**Official websites:** nccbarbados.gov.bb (most detailed), nccbarbados.com (also official), gov.bb/State-Bodies/national-conservation-commission

**Beach/park event permit process:**
- Written request to the General Manager at least 3 weeks before event
- Request must state: nature of activity, date, time, expected patron count
- NCC responds within 2 weeks of receiving full information

**Document requirements for beach/park events:**
- Police permits and COSCAP copyright licence required before NCC approval
- Security services from RBPF required if >200 patrons
- Chemical toilets required if >200 patrons (formula per Ministry of Health)
- Public Liability Insurance of BDS $100,000 for events with 100–1,000 patrons
- Fees apply for commercial structures (stages, tents, bars, stalls)

## Vendor licences at beaches and parks — confirmed 2026-05-28

**Governing statute:** National Conservation Commission Act, CAP 393 (proclaimed 1 April 1982). Anyone wishing to sell goods or services in a public park, garden, beach, or cave must obtain a licence from the NCC.

**Licence validity:** 1 year; renewable annually by 30 June.

**Non-transferable:** Licences are explicitly "not transferable."

**Renewal requirements (confirmed):**
- Recent Police Certificate of Character (all vendors)
- Health certificate (food/drink vendors only)
- Valid vessel registration + insurance certificates (watersports operators only)

**Renewal requirements (NOT confirmed from public web):**
- 2 referees (one professional, one personal) — not in any NCC public source
- 2 testimonials separate from referees — not in any NCC public source
- 2 passport-sized photos — not in any NCC public source

**Fees:** The NCC does not publish specific fee amounts on nccbarbados.com or the renewal notice. The renewal notice directs vendors to the Accounts Department for pricing. alpha.gov.bb lists $117.50 (regular), $176.25 (watersports), $11.75 (licence book), $12.87 (ID badge) — all consistent with standard 17.5% VAT but unverifiable from public sources.

**Accounts Department hours:** 8:30 a.m.–3:00 p.m., Monday–Friday (more restrictive than general office hours of 8:30 a.m.–4:30 p.m.).

**Post-approval documents:** Licence book + ID badge (both confirmed); "letter of authorisation" (not confirmed by any NCC source).

**alpha.gov.bb address error:** Page says "Codrington Road, Bridgetown, Saint Michael" — "Bridgetown" is wrong; should be "Waterford". See F-083.
