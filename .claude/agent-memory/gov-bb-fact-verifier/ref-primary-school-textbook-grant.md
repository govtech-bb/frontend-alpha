## Primary School $100 Textbook Grant — verified facts (2026-05-28)

**Programme:** Launched August 2023 by PM Mottley; administered by Ministry of Educational Transformation (MEDT).

**Amount:** BDS $100 per child (NOT "BDD $100" — "BDD" is a typo; correct is BDS$ or ISO code BBD).

**Eligibility:** Parents/guardians of students at public OR private primary schools. Excludes children already receiving support through the "Primary School Textbook system" (ministry's pre-existing means-tested book support for low-income families).

**Form rule:** ONE FORM PER CHILD (not one form per family per school). If a parent has three children, three forms are required. Alpha.gov.bb is wrong on this — the page says one form covers multiple children at the same school. Ministry and GIS sources confirm per-child forms.

**Principal role:** All forms route to the child's school. Principal is the authorising officer. Verified form then goes to MEDT Accounts Department for payment.

**Payment:** To bank or credit union account only (local accounts, BDS$ only). Name on account must match applicant name exactly. No bank account: Treasury can issue a voucher through the school principal.

**Receipts:** Initially required; October 2023 update confirmed waivable if an authorised teacher confirms new textbooks are present and principal verifies.

**Timing:** 2023 launch cycle: purchase window July 1 – September 30, 2023; application deadline October 16, 2023. Whether the programme runs annually on the same schedule is UNVERIFIED for 2024-2025 and 2025-2026.

**3-month bank rule on alpha.gov.bb (start.md):** Not corroborated by any Tier 1 or Tier 2 source. Flag as unverifiable.
