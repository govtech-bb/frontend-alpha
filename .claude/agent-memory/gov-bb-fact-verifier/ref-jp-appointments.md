## Justices of the Peace — Barbados Key Facts

**Appointing authority (post-republic):** The President of Barbados — confirmed by two swearing-in ceremonies after Barbados became a republic on 30 November 2021:
- December 2021: President Dame Sandra Mason presided (Wildey Gymnasium, ~400 JPs)
- January 2026: President Jeffrey Bostic presided (LESC)

**January 2026 swearing-in ceremony:**
- Date: Wednesday, 14 January 2026 (ceremony evening); reported 15 January 2026
- Venue: Lloyd Erskine Sandiford Centre, Two Mile Hill, St. Michael
- Count: **563** JPs sworn in (PM Mottley's own words when addressing them per Barbados Today)
- Note: GIS pre-ceremony announcement (13 Jan 2026) said 560 would be installed — the 563 figure is the post-event actual count
- Source: [Barbados Today — Hundreds take oath as new Justices of the Peace (15 Jan 2026)](https://barbadostoday.bb/2026/01/15/hundreds-take-oath-as-new-justices-of-the-peace/)

**October 2024 ceremony:**
- 43 new JPs sworn in
- Source: [Barbados Today — 43 new Justices of the Peace sworn in (16 Oct 2024)](https://barbadostoday.bb/2024/10/16/43-new-justices-of-the-peace-sworn-in/)
