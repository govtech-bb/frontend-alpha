## Programme facts (verified 2026-05-28)

- **Age eligibility:** 4–15 years old (confirmed by youthaffairs.gov.bb and Division Facebook posts)
- **Cost:** Free (confirmed by Division Facebook June 2025 and GIS search snippets)
- **Number of camps:** 46 locations in 2024 (youthaffairs.gov.bb about page); "approximately 50" on the 2024 dedicated page (likely rounding); "over 40" in search snippets. "~45" on alpha.gov.bb is within rounding.
- **Specialty camp types documented by Ministry:** entrepreneurship (YES Experience Enterprise), digital media/filmmaking (Next Steps Training Initiative), cultural arts, special needs (two programmes). "Sports" is NOT a named specialty type — sport activities appear in the general multi-activity programme.
- **Special needs:** Two dedicated special needs programmes (not just one as stated on alpha.gov.bb).
- **Camp dates (2024):** July 22 – August 23, 2024; **(2025):** July 21 – August 22, 2025.

## Registration timing (key discrepancy)

- **2025 cycle:** Registration announced 28 June 2025 with a 15 July 2025 deadline (gap of ~6 days before start, not 2 weeks). This contradicts the "approximately 2 weeks before start" claim on alpha.gov.bb.
- **"Registration opens in May" claim:** No authoritative source confirms May as the registration opening month. The 2025 cycle opened late June. As of 2026-05-28, no 2026 registration announcement has been made — the claim "Registration opens in May 2026" on alpha.gov.bb is likely wrong.
- Source: [Division of Youth Affairs Facebook — 2025 registration post (28 Jun 2025)](https://www.facebook.com/divisionofyouthbarbados/posts/a-new-hope-begins-this-summer-registration-is-now-open-for-the-national-summer-c/1032137589091244/)

---

## Volunteer recruitment timing (key discrepancy)

- **alpha.gov.bb claim:** "recruitment usually begins around April" — NOT supported by evidence.
- **2025 evidence:** Volunteer recruitment for 2025 was announced on 28 June 2025 (same day as camper registration), not April.
- **Volunteer age:** 16 or older — confirmed by Division Facebook and Instagram (June 2025 posts).
- **Roles:** Camp Assistant, General Camp Assistant, Camp Director.
- Source: [Division of Youth Affairs Facebook — 2025 volunteer post (28 Jun 2025)](https://www.facebook.com/divisionofyouthbarbados/posts/-are-you-16-or-older-and-ready-to-make-a-difference-this-summer-join-the-nationa/1032260372412299/)

## "Research Department" — unverifiable name

The Division of Youth Affairs does not list a "Research Department" in any public directory. A Research Officer staff role exists but is not a named department. Post-camp survey process is plausible but the department name in alpha.gov.bb content is unconfirmed. Consider rewording to "the Division of Youth Affairs."
