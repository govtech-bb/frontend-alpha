## Governing legislation
The Barbados Identity Management Act 2021 replaced the Statistics Act, Cap. 192 and the Statistics (Registration Census of Barbadian Residents) Regulations 1979 as the governing framework for national registration. The old programme ("National Registration") is superseded. The current card is the "Trident" digital ID.

## Eligibility
Citizens of Barbados OR non-nationals granted permission by the Chief Immigration Officer to remain for not less than six months (for work or study purposes). Confirmed in the Identity Management Act and on ebc.gov.bb/registration-information/.

## Fees (as at 2026-05-28)
- **First-time registration:** Free
- **Replacement card (lost/defaced/stolen/damaged/changed info/changed appearance):** BDS $60 — confirmed by GIS/CBC article (Feb 2023) and EBC PSA (Feb 2023); ebc.gov.bb/registration-information/ states "$60 for ALL replacement cards"
- **Expedited (same-day) replacement:** BDS $100
- **Age 65+ exemption:** replacement fee waived
- **16-year-olds converting minor → adult card:** free if old card returned; $60 if not
- NOTE: ebc.gov.bb/registration-2/ shows "$25" — this appears to be a stale page reflecting the pre-Trident fee. The current authoritative page (registration-information/) confirms $60.

## Photo requirements (all six — alpha.gov.bb page only lists two)
1. No hat, cap, shawl, scarf or any form of covering of the head or face (exception: religious reasons)
2. No tinted glasses, sunglasses, cosmetic eye covering, or hair obscuring the eyes
3. **No sleeveless clothing — shoulders must be covered** (MISSING from alpha.gov.bb page)
4. **No white clothing — photograph background is white** (MISSING from alpha.gov.bb page)
