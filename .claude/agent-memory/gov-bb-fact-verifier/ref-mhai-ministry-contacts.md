## Key findings from fact-check (2026-05-28)

### Apostrophe in "Webster's Business Park"
- Source content says "Webster Business Park" (no apostrophe) — matches gov.bb MHAI page
- OAG at the same building uses "Webster's Business Park" (with apostrophe)
- Recommend standardising all ministry entries to "Webster's Business Park"

### Meteorological Office — contested ministry attribution
- ministries.ts lists it under MHAI (Home Affairs cluster)
- gov.bb/Departments/meteorological-department attributes it to MIST
- agriculture.gov.bb hosts a "Barbados Meteorological Services" page under Agriculture (different entity: Charnocks, Christ Church; director Sonia Nurse; phone 535-0016)
- Barbados Parliament Estimates 2025–2026 PDF places Meteorology under Head 33 (MHAI)
- This is an unresolved governance conflict — needs ministry confirmation
- Source: https://www.barbadosparliament.com/uploads/document/6f738df0c1421b4c87131990813a6093.pdf

### Fire Service HQ — gov.bb stale (see also [[ref-bfs-new-headquarters]])
- gov.bb/Departments/fire-service still shows Level 5 GPO Building, Cheapside
- fireservice.gov.bb/contact/ shows CMM Emergency Services Complex, Prince Road, Pine Plantation Road, St. Michael as current HQ
- No alpha.gov.bb correction needed on MHAI page itself (no BFS address shown); but cross-reference if a BFS standalone page exists

### Temporary relocation (historical — May–July 2024)
- Ministry relocated to Worthing Corporate Centre, Worthing Main Road, Christ Church during renovations (21 May – 14 July 2024)
- Marriage Licence Office relocated to 4th Floor, Barbados Postal Service, Cheapside during same period
- Ministry has since returned to Jones Building; alpha.gov.bb page is correct
- Source: https://barbadostoday.bb/2024/05/15/temporary-relocation-of-the-ministry-of-home-affairs-and-information/

### "marriage license" spelling error on line 1
- Source content uses American "license" spelling; Barbados government uses British "licence" spelling throughout
- Full ministry name also truncated ("Ministry of Home Affairs" — should be "...and Information")
