Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade — verified 2026-05-28.

**Address:** 1 Culloden Road, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I. (confirmed by gov.bb, foreign.gov.bb, connectb1m.com; no named building in any Tier 1 source — the Frank Walcott Building on Culloden Road is the NISSS building, not the MFA building).

**Minister (as of 18 Feb 2026):** Senator The Hon. Christopher P. Sinckler — "Senior Minister of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade". Previously held by Kerrie D. Symmonds (now Minister of Energy, Business Development and Commerce). ministries.ts is correct.

**Contacts (all verified against gov.bb and foreign.gov.bb):**
- PBX: (246) 535-6620
- FA Fax: (246) 429-6652
- FT Fax: (246) 228-7840 (one non-authoritative source cites 228-0838 as an older alternate; ignore)
- Email: barbados@foreign.gov.bb
- Website: foreign.gov.bb

**Associated department:** "Consular and Diaspora Division" — name correct. Has 8 sub-divisions total per foreign.gov.bb/foreign-affairs/.

**Gov.bb Departments data error:** gov.bb/Departments/consular-diaspora shows WRONG address (Baobab Tower, Warrens — MIST building) and phone (535-1201). Every other source places the Division at 1 Culloden Road. Do not use gov.bb Departments page for this division's address. Flag to gov.bb team.
