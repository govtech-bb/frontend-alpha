The full canonical name per gov.bb/ministries (confirmed 2026-05-28) is "Ministry of the Public Service and Talent Development" (MPSTD). The ministry was established January 2019 through amalgamation of the Ministry of the Civil Service, Personnel Administration Division, and Training Administration Division. The amalgamation was passed 19 August 2019 under the Public Service (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 2019-35.

**Current minister (Feb 2026):** Kay S. McConney, M.P.
Source: barbadosparliament.com/page_content/show_content/8; barbadostoday.bb/2026/02/16/cabinet-ministers-sworn-in/

Three naming forms in circulation:
1. **gov.bb/ministries and ministries.ts (canonical):** "Ministry of the Public Service and Talent Development"
2. **mps.gov.bb header:** "Ministry of Public Service" (drops "the" and "and Talent Development")
3. **alpha.gov.bb Jobseekers page:** "Ministry of the Public Service" (drops "and Talent Development")
