The alpha.gov.bb Medical Requirements page (and its gov.bb source) names "The Diagnostic Clinic" on Beckles Road as the designated facility for US immigrant visa medical examinations in Barbados. This is outdated.

## Current authoritative source
US State Department BGN Bridgetown supplement (dated April 30, 2025):
- https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/Supplements/Supplements_by_Post/BGN-Bridgetown.html

Clapham Court, Wildey Main Road, St. Michael, Barbados, W.I. BB14007
Panel physicians: Dr. Sandra Reece, Dr. Ingrid Durrant
Walk-in: arrive by 7:00 a.m., Monday–Friday (no appointment needed)

**Location 2 (former Diagnostic Clinic):**
Corner of Jessamie Ave., Beckles Road, St. Michael, Barbados
Phone: (246) 426-5051 (also 426-5053 and 426-2423 in some directories)
Panel physicians: Dr. Roger Goddard, Dr. Anne Marie Irvine

## Key operational details (from BGN supplement)
- Exam fee: USD $200 (age 15+), USD $120 (under 15)
- Payment: cash, credit card, or electronic funds in USD or BDS
- Results turnaround: 72 hours (plan travel accordingly)
- Bring: visa appointment letter, passport, 4 colour photos, US residential address + zip code, email, immunisation records, DS-260 confirmation page, plus family doctor letter if medical history is relevant
- Clinic observes Barbadian holidays but remains open on US holidays

## Why gov.bb source is stale
The gov.bb Medical Requirements page (https://www.gov.bb/Visit-Barbados/medical-requirements) mirrors the alpha.gov.bb content verbatim and is also stale — both pages still say "The Diagnostic Clinic." The authoritative source is the US State Department BGN supplement, not gov.bb, for panel physician information.
