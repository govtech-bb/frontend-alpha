## Social Empowerment Agency (SEA)

**Launched:** 2 January 2026 (formal public launch 7 January 2026 at first client centre, Six Roads, St Philip)

**Merger:** Amalgamates Child Care Board, Welfare Department, National Assistance Board, National Disabilities Unit, and the Resilience and Reintegration Unit. Operates under the Ministry of People Empowerment and Elder Affairs.

**Head office (inherited from Welfare Department):** Weymouth Corporate Centre, Roebuck Street, Bridgetown, St. Michael. Phones: (246) 535-1000; (246) 535-1023; (246) 535-1005 (per GIS May 2024). Email: welfare.department@barbados.gov.bb (legacy — SEA-specific contacts unconfirmed on Tier 1 as at 2026-05-28).

**Client centres (new):**
- Six Roads, St Philip (opened 7 Jan 2026)
- Southern Plaza, Oistins, Christ Church (opened 27 Mar 2026)
- Additional centres planned for St Lucy (Bentham), St James (Sunset Crest), and at least 3 in St Michael

**Website:** socialempowermentbb.org (returning connection-refused intermittently as at 2026-05-28 — try gov.bb/Departments/welfare as a fallback until gov.bb updates)

**Gov.bb status:** gov.bb/Ministries/social-care and gov.bb/Departments/welfare have NOT been updated to reflect the SEA — still list "Welfare Department". Do not treat gov.bb as authoritative for agency name until updated.

**Disaster relief mandate:** Social workers formally designated as "first responders" under the January 2026 reform. The Resilience and Reintegration Unit is now inside SEA, covering disaster social response.
