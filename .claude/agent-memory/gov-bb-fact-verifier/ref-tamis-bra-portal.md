## TAMIS — Tax Administration Management Information System

**Full name:** Tax Administration Management Information System (TAMIS)

**Portal URL:** https://tamis.bra.gov.bb/ — confirmed live 2026-05-28; tagline "Tax made easy!"

**What TAMIS handles (not income tax only):**
Phase 1 (June 2018): Corporate Income Tax, PAYE, Withholding Tax, Premium Tax, Tax on Bank Assets, VAT, NSRL, Excise Tax.
Phase 2 (Feb 2019): Personal Income Tax added.
Also: Betting and Gaming Fees (confirmed bra.gov.bb/Pay/Income-Tax); Land Tax is a separate portal.

**Help desk contacts (confirmed via tamis.bra.gov.bb and TAMIS Key Dates page):**
- Phone: (246) 535-8239 (direct TAMIS help) / (246) 429-ETAX = (246) 429-3829 (general BRA line)
- Email: tamis@bra.gov.bb / bramail@bra.gov.bb
- Hours: 8:30am to 4:30pm, Monday to Friday (excluding public holidays)

**Individual income tax filing deadline:** April 30 (confirmed bra.gov.bb/Popular-Topics/Self-Employment/Filing-Your-Tax-Return)
