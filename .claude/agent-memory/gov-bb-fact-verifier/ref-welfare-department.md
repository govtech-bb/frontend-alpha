## Welfare Department — verified facts (2026-05-28)

**Ministry:** Ministry of People Empowerment and Elder Affairs (MPEA) — https://www.gov.bb/Ministries/social-care

**Head office:**
- Weymouth Corporate Centre (GIS/British spelling), also written "Weymouth Corporate Center" (American, used on gov.bb) — at the corner of Country Road and Roebuck Street, Bridgetown, St. Michael
- Source: https://www.gov.bb/Departments/welfare

**Phone numbers (verified):**
- (246) 535-1000 (main PBX)
- (246) 535-1005 (per GIS May 2024 direct deposit release)
- (246) 535-1023 (second PBX)
- Fax: (246) 535-1006
- **The "/16/" in alpha.gov.bb format `+1 246-535-1000/16/23` is unverifiable — no extension 16 appears in any official source.**

**Email:**
- General: welfare.department@barbados.gov.bb
- Accounts/payments: welfare.accounts@barbados.gov.bb (from GIS May 2024 release)

**Branch offices (per GIS 2017 telephone directory):**
- Speightstown, Saint Peter: **BNB Building**, Chapel Street (NOT "Republic Bank Building" — BNB acquired by Republic Bank 2010 but government retains BNB name)
  - St. Peter office direct: (246) 535-1049
  - St. James office (same building): (246) 535-1048
- Country Road, St. Michael: **opposite the National Housing Corporation**

**EFT / Direct deposit — LIVE as of 21 May 2024:**
- GIS press release: https://gisbarbados.gov.bb/blog/national-assistance-grants-now-via-direct-deposit/
- National Assistance Grants are now paid by direct deposit to bank/credit union account
- Banking details form: is.gd/WelfareBenefitsForm
- Welfare Dept helps recipients without accounts acquire one at no cost
- **alpha.gov.bb claim "electronic transfer of funds is not available yet" is WRONG — discrepant, Confidence 95%**

**National Assistance Act:**
- Cap. 48 in Laws of Barbados — PDF at https://www.barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/assets/content/pdfs/statutes/NationalAssistanceCAP048.pdf
- Governing statute for welfare assistance; "National Assistance framework" on the page correctly refers to this Act

**Leadership:** Ms. Joan Prescod, Chief Welfare Officer (Acting) — as of 2026-05-28 per gov.bb

**Related body:** National Assistance Board — Murrell House, Country Road, St. Michael; PBX (246) 535-3131 — separate from Welfare Department, also under MPEA
