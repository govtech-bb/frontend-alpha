## Notaries Public Act — Barbados

**Current Act:** Notaries Public Act, 2024 — came into force **1 September 2024**, replacing the 2017-09 Act.

**Eligibility under 2024 Act:**
- Attorneys-at-law in good standing with the Barbados Bar Association (15+ years practice)
- Chartered accountants in good standing with a recognised accounting body (15+ years practice) — NEW under 2024 Act
- Persons with other professional/business experience (15+ years) — NEW under 2024 Act; training course required

**Previous Act:** Notaries Public Act, 2017-09 — did NOT include chartered accountants; notarial services were limited to a small number of public officers at the registrar level.

**Impact on fact-checking:**
Any content referencing the Notaries Public Act should cite the 2024 Act, not the 2017 Act. The "chartered accountant" description on justice-of-the-peace.md is correct under the 2024 Act. The get-a-document-notarised.md page may have references to the 2017 Act that need updating.
