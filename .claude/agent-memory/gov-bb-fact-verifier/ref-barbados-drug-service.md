## Special Benefit Service (SBS)

**Eligibility:** Barbadian citizens and permanent residents with a prescription on the Barbados National Drug Formulary.

**Free at polyclinic and zero dispensing fee at private pharmacy:**
- Aged 65 or over
- Under 16 years old
- Prescribed for: asthma, cancer, diabetes, epilepsy, glaucoma, or hypertension

**Dispensing fees at private SBS pharmacies (effective April 1, 2024):**
- Three tiers: $7 / $10 / $14 per item (increased from $5 / $7 / $12)
- Source: [GIS — Fee Increase For Certain Prescriptions Under Special Benefit Service](https://gisbarbados.gov.bb/blog/fee-increase-for-certain-prescriptions-under-special-benefit-service/); [Nation News 24 March 2024](https://nationnews.com/2024/03/24/special-benefit-service-beneficiaries-fees-to-go-up/)
- Note: The GIS page returns 403 on WebFetch; confirmed via Google-indexed snippet and Nation News reporting.
- The which-drug-pays-which-tier breakdown is NOT publicly documented — only the three tier change amounts are confirmed.

## Polyclinic count

BDS pharmacies operate in **9 polyclinics, 3 outpatient clinics, 2 district hospitals** (from BDS pharmacy service page).
Ministry of Health lists **9 polyclinics + 2 satellite clinics** = 11 total health facilities.

The figure "12 government polyclinic locations" on open-pharmacy.md is wrong — no source supports 12.
Source: [Ministry of Health — Primary Health Care](https://www.health.gov.bb/For-Public/Primary-Health-Care)

The 9 polyclinics are: Branford Tait (St. Michael), Maurice Byer (St. Peter), Winston Scott (St. Michael), Eunice Gibson (St. Michael), David Thompson Health & Social Services Complex (St. John), Glebe (St. George), Randal Phillips (Christ Church), Edgar Cochrane (St. Michael), St. Philip (St. Philip).
Satellite clinics: St. Joseph Outpatient Clinic, St. Andrew Outpatient Clinic.

## Formulary categories

- **Category A:** Free to all eligible beneficiaries at point of service
- **Category B:** Requires prior approval from Medical Officer of Health + Consultant through Chairman, Drug & Therapeutics Committee (QEH) or Director BDS. Free in public sector once approved.
- **Category C:** Patient pays at subsidized (VAT-free, duty-free) cost in private sector

Source: [Formulary Categories](https://formulary.drugservice.gov.bb/bndf2022/formulary_categories_dashboard.php)
