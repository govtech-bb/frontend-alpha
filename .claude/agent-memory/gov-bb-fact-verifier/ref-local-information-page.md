Fact-checked 2026-05-28. Source file: `src/content/local-information.md`. Live page: `https://alpha.gov.bb/travel-id-citizenship/local-information`. Source URL `https://www.gov.bb/Visit-Barbados/local-information` confirmed live and matching.

**Key finding — F-050:** "Ranked highly in most global population indexes" is wrong. "Population indexes" rank countries by population size; Barbados is ~186th in the world (~282,000 people). The intended meaning is quality-of-life/development indices (HDI, Passport Index, etc.) where Barbados does rank well. Confidence it is wrong: 90%.

**Verified tourism claims:** Barbados is legitimately "one of the top Caribbean travel destinations" — confirmed by: Nat Geo Best of World 2025 (tourism.gov.bb press release), Star Luxury Destination Award 2024 (Travel Bulletin), Best Tourist Board 2024 (Globe Travel Awards, only Caribbean country). "Receives a great deal of press coverage" is verified by multiple recent official recognitions.

**Barbados international index rankings (from stats.gov.bb/international-indices/ and UNDP 2025 HDR):**
- HDI: rank 69/189 (Very High Human Development) per 2025 HDR
- Passport Index: rank 22/199
- Corruption Perceptions Index: rank 30/198
- Gender Development Index: rank 12/189
- Freedom in the World: rank 13/210
- ICT Development Index: rank 34/176

**Grammar issue noted (editorial only):** "one of the top Caribbean travel destination" should be "destinations" (plural). No triage card raised — editorial only.
