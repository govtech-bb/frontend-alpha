Per BRA — Public Entertainment (https://bra.gov.bb/Popular-Topics/Public-Entertainment/), four categories are exempt from the Promoter's Authorisation requirement:

1. "an approved educational institution"
2. "the board of management or a parent teacher association of an approved educational institution"
3. "a person who provides entertainment on a daily or weekly basis"
4. "a church registered under the Charities Act"

The alpha.gov.bb loud-music-permit page (src/content/loud-music-permit.md lines 53–55) lists only:
- educational institutions
- religious groups

Two exemptions are missing (#2 PTAs, #3 daily/weekly providers). The "religious groups" phrasing does not match the statutory "a church registered under the Charities Act" — some religious organisations would not qualify under the narrower statutory definition.

Confidence it's wrong: 80% (discrepant, Tier B finding).
