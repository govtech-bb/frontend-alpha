## NIS Severance Department — verified 2026-05-28

- **Phone:** (246) 431-7400 (NIS main switchboard)
- **Extensions for Severance:** Ext. 1502 to 1509 (per nis.gov.bb/contact-us/)
- **Alternative phone:** (246) 467-4647 (customer service)
- **Email:** severancedepartment@bginis.gov.bb

### Official department name
"Severance Department" (NOT "Severance Payment Department") — confirmed by NIS Contact Us page and the email slug. The word "Payment" is not used by NIS in any page title or label.

### Key eligibility criteria (per nis.gov.bb/severance/)
1. At least **104 continuous weeks** (= 2 years) with the same employer
2. **Over 16 and under pensionable age** (pensionable age = 67, effective 1 January 2018) at date of termination
3. Contracted for **no less than 21 hours per week**
4. Terminated for: redundancy, laid-off, kept on short-time, or because of a natural disaster
5. **Death of employer** — present in Cap. 355A (section heading confirmed); NOT listed in NIS's four qualifying events on nis.gov.bb/severance/

### Claim deadline
1 year (12 months) from date of termination. Barbados Today (19 Sep 2020) cites Labour Department confirmation. There is also a 4-week employer-notification requirement before the 12-month written claim submission.

### Calculation formula (per NIS)
- **Basis:** average weekly **basic pay** over the last 104 weeks — NOT gross pay
  - Basic pay explicitly excludes: overtime, commission on sales, service charge, production bonus (these are "insurable earnings" but not "basic pay")
- **Rate by years of service:**
  - 0–10 years: 2.5 weeks' basic pay per completed year
  - 10–20 years: 3.0 weeks' basic pay per completed year
  - 20–33 years: 3.5 weeks' basic pay per completed year
  - Max service counted: 33 years
- **Salary cap:** Maximum insurable earnings ceiling ($1,201/week as of January 1, 2024; $5,360/month as of January 1, 2026 per third-party payroll sources)

### NIS Severance Fund
Employers who pay severance can claim a 25% rebate from the NIS Severance Fund within 6 months, with supporting documentation.
