## Project Protégé — verified facts (2026-05-28)

**Programme:** Project Protégé — a mentorship programme for youth aged 13–17, pairing them with volunteer mentors for a six-month period.

**Ministry:** Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment (MYSCE). Launched 30 January 2023 by MYSCE. **Not** the Ministry of Labour, Social Security and Third Sector.

**Address:** Sky Mall, Haggatt Hall, St. Michael (verified via gov.bb and youthaffairs.gov.bb/contact-us/)

**Phone:** (246) 535-3835

**YDP email:** YDP@barbados.gov.bb — confirmed via gov.bb PDF document; not listed on a currently-rendering .gov.bb web page. Treat as 65% confident.

**Sub-programme:** Project Protégé sits under the Youth Development Programme (YDP), which is a channel of the Division of Youth Affairs.

**Division branding note:** youthaffairs.gov.bb now identifies itself as "Division of Youth & Culture, Prime Minister's Office" — not purely "Division of Youth Affairs". The ministry (MYSCE) still has the site branded as Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment.

**projectprotege.gov.bb:** Does not exist (ECONNREFUSED 2026-05-28).

**Police Certificate of Character:**
- URL: https://forms.gov.bb/CertificateOfCharacter (live, confirmed 2026-05-28)
- Fee: BBD $20
- Processing: 24–48 hours after payment
- Office: Inga Lodge, Pinfold Street, St. Michael

**ministries.ts internal inconsistency:** "Apply to be a Project Protégé Mentor" is listed under `onlineServices` for `ministry-of-labour-social-security-and-third-sector` (lines 806–809). It should be under `ministry-of-youth-sports-and-community-empowerment`.
