## Ministry of Defence and Security — status as of 2026-05-28

**Current status:** "Ministry of Defence and Security" does NOT appear in the current gov.bb listing of ministries (checked 2026-05-28 at gov.bb/ministries). GIS has used this name in press releases historically, but it is not in the current ministerial list.

**Current portfolio holder:** Defence and security matters fall under the Prime Minister's portfolio (PM Mia Amor Mottley holds "Minister of National Security"). The PMO is at Government Headquarters, Bay Street, St. Michael.

**Barbados Defence Force (BDF):** Located at St. Ann's Fort, The Garrison, St. Michael. Phone: 536-2500 (main). This is confirmed on gov.bb.

**defence.gov.bb:** Does NOT resolve (ECONNREFUSED, 2026-05-28). Do not use as a link.

**Correct link for BDF-related content:** https://www.gov.bb/State-Bodies/defence-force

**Pyrotechnics/fireworks permitting:** Involves both BDF (explosives approval) and BFS (fire safety certificate). No single published process document found on the open web.
