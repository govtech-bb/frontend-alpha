Under the Liquor Licence Act, 2021 (online system launched 1 April 2022), the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs (DCCA) took over administrative responsibility for issuing liquor licences. The Liquor Licensing Authority now administers applications.

- Applications submitted at: https://liquorlicence.gov.bb/
- Payment via EZpay+ platform (debit/credit card)
- Help Desk: Warrens Office Complex, Warrens, St. Michael; tel 535-7011 or 535-7019; email commerce.ca@barbados.gov.bb
- Hours: Mon–Fri 8:30 a.m.–4:15 p.m.
- Processing target: ~7 days (vs. ~5 weeks under the old Magistracy system)

The Commissioner of Police is NOTIFIED of applications (section 11(1)) and may appear at objection hearings (section 13) — but plays NO role in receiving or processing applications.
