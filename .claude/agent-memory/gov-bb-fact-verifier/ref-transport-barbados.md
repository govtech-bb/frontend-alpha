## Transport Board (transportboard.com)

- State-owned bus operator. Motto: "You are in safe hands with us." Marked 70 Years of Service December 2025.
- **Adult fare: BBD $3.50** (exact change only — no change given from over $3.50). Confirmed current as of November 2025.
- **Free travel:** school children under 18 in uniform; old-age pensioners with valid ID; law enforcement officers; Transport Board employees. Children under 5 free (cannot occupy a seat).
- **Reduced fare:** $2.50 for school children under 18 on minibuses and route taxis.
- Contact: +1246-535-3500; customerservice@transportboard.com; Weymouth, Roebuck Street, St. Michael.
- Route finder: https://www.transportboard.com/route-finder/ (live 2026-05-28).

## Transport Authority (ta.gov.bb)

Separate body from Transport Board. Manages licensing/permits for private operators (minibuses, ZRs).

- Route and schedule database: https://ta.gov.bb/Routes/
- **Route taxi (ZR) routes:** 31 routes listed as of 2026-05-28. Numbers include 01–12, 15, 17, 19, 61 and lettered variants (1A, 1B, 1D, 3C, 3D, 3E, 3L, 3M, 3N, 3O, 3Q, 3R, 3V, 3W, 8B). The historic "1 through 11" description is outdated.
- Minibus routes: numbered 13–99 and lettered (3G, 3H, 3I, etc.).

## Minibuses (PSVs)

- **Appearance:** bright yellow with a horizontal blue stripe (national colours reversed).
- Smaller than Transport Board buses; privately owned; driver and conductor.
- **Fare: BBD $3.50** (same as Transport Board).
- Run select routes; final destination displayed in bottom-left of windshield.

## ZR Vans (Route Taxis)

- **Appearance:** white minivans with maroon stripe; letters ZR on licence plate.
- Privately owned; run specific numbered routes.
- **Routes:** 31 officially listed by ta.gov.bb (NOT "1 through 11" as alpha.gov.bb states — that description is outdated).
- Terminals: Cheapside Terminal and River Road Terminal (Bridgetown), Oistins terminal.

## Taxi Cabs

- No meter system — fare must be agreed before journey.
- Fares are government-regulated. Most recent regulated increase effective **7 January 2023** (after 15 years without increase), announced by Deputy PM / Minister of Transport Santia Bradshaw.
- Island tours offered at flat rates (common commercial practice; no government-set flat-tour rate found).
