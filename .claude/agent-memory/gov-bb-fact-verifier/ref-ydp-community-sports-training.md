YDP Community Sports Training programme is run by the Division of Youth Affairs under the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment (MYSCE).

**Age range:** The YDP's official mandate is nine (9) to twenty-nine (29). The alpha.gov.bb page says "30 and under" — this is DISCREPANT (F-072). Confidence 85%.

**Ministry address:** Sky Mall, Haggatt Hall, St. Michael — verified across gov.bb, youthaffairs.gov.bb, departments.ts, ministries.ts.

**Phone:** (246) 535-3835 (main PBX) — confirmed five independent sources (Tier 1).

**Principal Youth Development Officer:** real title, confirmed via LinkedIn (John Hollingsworth) and civil service salary schedules; extension reportedly 535-3860.

**Youth Commissioners:** real roles — 32 districts across 4 zones, each with a Youth Commissioner.

**YDP programme link:** `https://youthaffairs.gov.bb/programme-channels/youth-development-programme/` — URL is live but as of 2026-05-28 shows only non-sports content (cyber security, arts, web design, entrepreneurship). No sports training listed (F-073).

**Sports training programme evidence:** youthaffairs.gov.bb About page lists "Community sports and cultural training programmes" as an annual activity. YDP Sports Rally article confirms 10-week cycle July–September 2022, 668 participants, 43 island locations. Barbados Today (Mar 2025) confirms 668 in current financial year.

**"New programmes begin throughout the year":** unverifiable — the evidence points to an annual structured cycle, not rolling intake.

**Triage items from this page:** F-072 (age eligibility discrepant), F-073 (YDP link useless for sports)
