## Key verified facts (as of 2026-05-28)

- **28-day deadline**: confirmed on gov.bb/register-birth and barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb
- **Late registration fee**: BDS $20.00 — confirmed on gov.bb/register-birth and gov.bb/Citizens/register-birth
- **Certified copy fee**: BDS $5.00 (standard), BDS $1.00 (age 60+) — confirmed on barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/useful-links/for-public/how-to-order/certificates AND gov.bb/register-birth AND gov.bb/Citizens/register-birth (three Tier 1 sources agree)
- **Registration is free** — no fee for the act of registering, only for the certificate
- **Turnaround for new registrations**: "2 working days" (gov.bb); same-day if submitted before 11:30am
- **Turnaround for standalone copy requests (not tied to new registration)**: NOT documented on any authoritative public source. "5 to 7 business days" phrasing on alpha.gov.bb get-birth-certificate page is unverifiable — the same wording appears verbatim on the alpha.gov.bb death certificate page, suggesting possible copy-paste error. Requires agency confirmation.
- **Hours**: 8:30am–3:30pm, Monday to Friday (gov.bb/register-birth + gov.bb/Citizens/register-birth). The alpha.gov.bb page incorrectly says 3:15pm — this originated in a May 2020 COVID-era temporary schedule (Barbados Today 2020-05-17), not the current standard hours.
- **1-year grace period**: NOT confirmed by any authoritative source — gov.bb only states late fee applies after 28 days; the 1-year threshold is unverifiable from public web
- **Governing Act**: Vital Statistics Registration Act, Cap. 192A (PDF at barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/assets/content/pdfs/statutes/VitalStatisticsRegistrationCAP192A.pdf — binary, not machine-readable)
- **Minor parent requirement**: gov.bb says: Minor's ID Card + passport-size picture certified by JP + Birth Certificate OR Passport. Alpha.gov.bb incorrectly says "identification letter signed by JP" (should be a passport-size picture certified by JP).
- **Overseas direct-dial +1 (246) 535-9751**: appears on alpha.gov.bb birth and death certificate pages, but NOT confirmed on barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb or gov.bb/Departments/registration. Needs agency confirmation. The Tier 1 phone is 1-246-535-9700; fax is 1-246-426-2405.
- **"Place of baptism" on birth certificate application form**: confirmed as a genuine form field (Wikiprocedure cites the official PDF). Reflects Barbados's historical ecclesiastical/civil registration practice. Not an error on the alpha.gov.bb page.
- **Same-day emergency provision for birth certificate copies**: not confirmed on any Tier 1 source for birth certificates. Appears verbatim on death certificate page — possible copy-paste from death cert content.

## Watch out for

- GIS Barbados blog pages frequently return HTTP 403 on WebFetch — use WebSearch to surface content from these URLs
- gov.bb/Departments/registration gives email as registrar@lawcourts.gov.bb; barbadoslawcourts.gov.bb/certificates gives registrarsupremecourt@barbados.gov.bb — two different email addresses for the same department; confirm which is current
- "Eric Holder Municipal Complex" (on the page) vs. "Eric Holder Jr. Municipal Complex" (official) — the "Jr." is consistently omitted in alpha.gov.bb content
