## President of Barbados (as of 2026-05-28)

- **Current officeholder:** Lieutenant Colonel the Most Honourable Jeffrey Davidson Bostic
- **Took office:** 30 November 2025 (59th Independence Anniversary)
- **Predecessor:** The Most Honourable Dame Sandra Mason (2021–2025)
- **Formal style (presidential):** "The Most Honourable Jeffrey Bostic" or in full "Lieutenant Colonel the Most Honourable Jeffrey Bostic"
- **Military rank:** Lieutenant Colonel (Barbados Defence Force, retired); rank is used before the presidential honorific in formal style

## State House / Office of the President

- **Address:** State House, Government Hill, St. Michael, Barbados
- **Location confirmed by:** Wikipedia, Wikidata (coords 13°6'4.32"N 59°35'58.56"W), Waze, barbados.org — all confirm Government Hill, St. Michael
- **No `.gov.bb` postal-address source verified** — presidentofbarbados.org returned no content in May 2026
- **gov.bb/Visit-Barbados/president was stale as of 2026-05-28** — still showed Dame Sandra Mason profile; escalate to Cabinet Office

## Centenarian visit programme

- **Practice confirmed:** President visits centenarians at their homes on their 100th birthday — documented in multiple GIS and Barbados Today/CBC articles 2021–2026
- **Recent visits under Bostic:** Muriel Dilworth (March 2026, Barbados Today); Ealing Park centenarian (January 2026, Barbados Today)
- **Nation News article (Nov 2025):** President Mason invited 31 centenarians to State House; "most … were visited on their 100th birthday"
